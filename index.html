About Us

Located in Sanepa, Lalitpur, Maxims Banquet & Events is a professional venue for celebrating special occasions. Opened in October 2018, the banquet hall is a 10-minute walk from Pulchowk Road and is situated opposite the Summit Hotel, Sanepa. The venue is known for its excellent service and high-quality buffets, making it a popular choice for events such as colleague parties and social gatherings.
